Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

The plain-language version

We built LINEOG because we were tired of choosing between useful AI and private AI. Here's the short version of how we handle your information:

  • What we collect: what you type into the recommendation tool, and basic, anonymous usage data (like how many people visit, and what pages they view).
  • What we don't collect: your name, unless you give it to us; passwords or account credentials for any other service; advertising identifiers.
  • Why we collect it: to generate your recommendation, and to understand what people are asking so we can improve it.
  • What we never do: sell your prompts, use them to build an advertising profile, or share them with anyone beyond what's needed to generate your recommendation.
  • If you give us your email to hear about future LINEOG features, we use it only to reach out about that, nothing else.
  • How to reach us about your information: hello@lineog.com.

1. Who we are

LINEOG ("we," "us," "our") operates lineog.com and its related tools, including the AI recommendation tool (the "Service"). This policy explains what information the Service collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide directly:

  • The text you enter describing a task, in order to receive an AI recommendation and an optimized prompt.
  • Your name and email address, if you choose to share them to express interest in future LINEOG features.

Information collected automatically:

  • Standard, privacy-oriented analytics (page views, referring source, device type) via Vercel Analytics, which does not use cookies and does not track visitors across other websites.
  • Basic technical logs generated by our hosting provider (Vercel) as part of normal web infrastructure operation.

3. How we use information

  • To generate and display an AI recommendation and optimized prompt in response to what you type.
  • To understand, in aggregate, what kinds of tasks people are asking about, so we can improve the tool.
  • To contact you about LINEOG's future product, only if you've shared your email for that purpose.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the Service.

4. What we don't do

  • We do not sell the text you enter, or any information collected through the Service, to third parties.
  • We do not use what you type to build advertising profiles, and we do not run third-party advertising on the Service.
  • We do not use your submitted tasks to train any AI model. Our AI provider processes each request only to generate your recommendation, under their standard API terms, which exclude that data from model training.

5. Third parties involved in providing the Service

To generate a recommendation, the text you enter is sent to Anthropic's API (which powers Claude) for the sole purpose of producing that recommendation. Anthropic's standard API terms govern that processing and exclude the data from being used to train their models. We use Vercel for hosting and privacy-oriented analytics. When you choose to open a recommended AI assistant (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, or Grok), you leave the Service and interact with that provider directly, under their own privacy policy and terms, which we don't control.

6. Data retention

Task text submitted to the recommendation tool is retained in system logs for a limited operational period, no more than 30 days, for debugging and service improvement, after which it is not separately retained by us. If you submit your name or email to express interest in LINEOG, we retain that information until you ask us to delete it or until it's no longer needed for the purpose you provided it.

7. Your choices

  • You are never required to provide your name or email to use the recommendation tool itself.
  • You can ask us to delete any information associated with an email you've shared with us by writing to hello@lineog.com.
  • If you are located in the European Union, the UK, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or the CCPA, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to opt out of certain uses of it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@lineog.com and we will respond within the time required by applicable law.

8. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we've collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your information: hello@lineog.com.
